Scrambled Eggs Receipe

Description

This receipe goes over the details in creating a quick dish of Scrambled eggs. Scrambled eggs is a quick and fast breakfast that will keep you full for a few hours.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons sliced Cheddar cheese
  • 2 thin slices ham (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon butter

Receipe Steps

  1. Whisk eggs together in a small bowl until smooth. Mix in Cheddar cheese, ham, and heavy cream.
  2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Pour in egg mixture; cook and stir until set but still moist, 3 to 5 minutes.
